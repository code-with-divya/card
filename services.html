Nature

The words nature and natural are used for all the things that are normally not made by humans. The word comes from the Latin natura meaning "birth". Nature includes many things like weather, organisms, landforms, celestial bodies. Scientists study the way the parts of nature work. Nature is a common notion, which everyone is familiar with as long as we are not asked to define it.

Night Nature

Life at Night Atlas is a National Geographic Society project, directed by science photojournalist Babak Tafreshi. It brings together an archive of visual stories on wildlife at night, while creating a team of night photographers, filmmakers, biologists, and light pollution specialists to produce new stories on how various species relate to the night sky and dark environments.
